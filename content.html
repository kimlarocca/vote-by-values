Issue Breakdown

Education Rollbacks & DOE Dismantling

Jack is terrified to teach real history—slavery, Jim Crow, labor battles, civil-rights protests vanish from classrooms. He echoes Trump’s push to gut the U.S. Department of Education, which today:

  • Manages Title I grants for low-income schools
  • Enforces IDEA protections for special-needs students
  • Conducts civil-rights investigations against discrimination
  • Provides teacher training and literacy interventions

Under Jack, Trenton would scramble to fill those gaps or jack up state taxes—ask Elon how DOGE’s doing, or ask Republicans if they still call themselves fiscal conservatives after exploding the federal deficit.

Private Prisons & Jan. 6 Pardons

Jack preaches “law and order”—while cheering private-prison profiteers and staying stone-cold silent when Trump pardoned Jan. 6 rioters who beat and murdered Officer Brian Sicknick. If he “loves the Blue,” why cover for cop killers?

Masked ICE Raids & Due Process Hypocrisy

Jack champions “defend due process” only for gun owners; on immigration, he backs masked ICE agents snatching neighbors without warrants—eroding the very rights he touts elsewhere.

Social Security & Medicare Betrayal

“Jack will never support cuts to Social Security or Medicare for seniors”? His party in Washington just rammed a budget slashing $880 billion from Medicare/Medicaid and teeing up Social Security cuts. When will Jack stand up?

Authoritarian Aid Withholding

Jack warns that cities “who disobey could have municipal aid withheld from Trenton until they comply.” Strongman tactics straight from Trump’s playbook—using public dollars as political cudgels.

Public Health & Vaccine Rollbacks

He’d gut scientific advisory panels, block vaccine mandates under broad religious exemptions, and starve routine immunization programs—setting NJ up to lose the next pandemic.
